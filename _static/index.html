Ideal project process

Our ideal approach to engage with our projects and leave a great impressions.

1. Meet / Chat

The first step is to discover more about you, your company's vision, and to build a strong relationship. This is where we will create your brand, create a custom strategy, and set project goals.

2. Plan

Following the initial meet up, we will outline your project, create milestones, and agree on priorities. Now we have a strategic plan in place that aligns with your initial vision and makes your goals achievable.

3. Wireframe & Design

By building a rough outline of your site (black and white, no graphics) we ensure that every piece is in place. At this point, if there are any content changes, they can easily be made without disrupting the design. Once the wireframe is finished, visual concepts or design of the custom project will be created.

4. Develop & Coding

Then start the project coding - "More coffee will be used here!". Our development team develop and revises the project files or sources until it aligns with your goals.

5. Testing & QA

Here review and testing takes place, which ensures the quality of your project. This is the most valuable step because your reputation is our reputation!

6. Launch

Here is where we present your custom project. Then sit back and watch the stunning result!

Note: Above process might change without prior notice in order to match the clients needs.
